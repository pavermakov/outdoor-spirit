ABOUT THE COMPANY

WE ARE… the OUTDOOR SPIRIT™, one of the largest sport and tourism goods chain! Our roots go back to 1937 in Columbus, OH, when a small shop called Dave & Richard's opened. Over the years with hard work, great customer service and attention to detail we have grown into a full line goods chain serving customers in 11 countries, from USA to China. Every one of our over 200 stores worldwide offers a full line of traditional sport and tourism goods and athletic equipment as well as a wide variety of active and casual sports apparel and footwear. Our motto at the OUTDOOR SPIRIT™ is Big Adventure…Low Prices. That is what we do everyday, give our customers the choice of the name brands that they want, and give them to them at the lowest prices possible. You will notice this in every store you go into where you will find Hot Deals and Values at prices that may be too low to advertise. Stop in for one of our awesomely low priced ad items and may walk out with a couple of hot in-store deals as well. We are dedicated to you, and helping you take your adventure to the next level. If this is what you have been looking for in a sport and tourism goods retailer, then you've come to the right store!
